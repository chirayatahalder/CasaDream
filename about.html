About us

Our Story

Casa dream is a multi-brand furniture outlet in India. We present you with an array of curiously picked collections from over 100 brands from across the world through our network of 25 cities. Casa dream specializes in fusing creative interior design with engineering efficiency to create magnificent living spaces that exude elegance and life from every inch of their work. Casa Dream was established in 2016, when there was no presence of any branded furniture or furnishing in the region. Casa Dream has since remained THE Destination fro the discerning and quality conscious buyer. Casa Dream community is the one which appreciates the finer side of living, simple and daily luxuries. Since its inception, Casa Dream has helped homeowners, architects and interior designers to achieve their vision of their home, with its finest products and services. A truly one-stop destination for homes and offices with bathroom fittings, kitchens, wardrobes, furniture, furnishings & decor under one roof with the presence of an amazing and talented team.
